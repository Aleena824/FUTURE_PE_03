## Why SEO Pricing Varies So Much

SEO pricing in Dubai ranges from AED 1,500 to AED 50,000+ per month. That's not a typo. The variation comes down to what's actually included.

**Cheap SEO packages (under AED 3,000/month)** typically cover basic on-page fixes and a handful of low-quality backlinks. They rarely move the needle for competitive keywords and sometimes use tactics that can get your site penalised by Google.

**Mid-range packages (AED 5,000–15,000/month)** include proper keyword research, technical SEO, content creation, and link building. This is where most Dubai SMEs should be looking.

**Premium SEO retainers (AED 15,000+/month)** cover enterprise-level strategy, dedicated teams, advanced analytics, and competitive keyword targeting in high-value sectors like real estate or finance.

---

## What Should Be Included in Any SEO Package

Regardless of budget, a legitimate SEO package should include:

**Keyword Research** — identifying what your target customers actually search for, not just what sounds good.

**On-Page Optimisation** — improving your website's title tags, meta descriptions, headings, and content structure.

**Technical SEO** — ensuring your site loads fast, is mobile-friendly, and can be properly crawled by Google.

**Content Creation** — blogs, service pages, and landing pages that target your keywords.

**Monthly Reporting** — clear data showing what changed, what improved, and what's next.

If an agency can't tell you exactly what's included in their package, that's a problem.

## Is SEO Worth It for Dubai Businesses?

For most businesses, yes — but only if you're patient. SEO is a long-term investment. You won't see results in week one. Most businesses start seeing meaningful movement at the 3–6 month mark, with significant results by month 9–12.

The payoff is that organic traffic, unlike paid ads, doesn't stop when you stop paying. A well-optimised page can generate leads for years.
